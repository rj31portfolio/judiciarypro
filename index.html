About JudiciaryPRO

About Us

JudiciaryPRO is the next chapter of a journey that began in 2013 when Sparsh Jain founded Chinar Law Institute in Gurugram and led it until the end of 2023. Over a decade, that classroom-first ethos, clarity in concepts, discipline in practice, and personal mentorship produced 50+ selections and a community of serious learners. JudiciaryPRO carries that legacy forward with a modern, hybrid model (online + offline), daily doubt-clearing, mains answer writing, weekly tests, bilingual teaching (English/Hindi), library access, and a current-affairs routine. We do not just cover law, we build the habit of reading, reasoning, and writing like a judge because ignorantia juris non excusat and every citizen should know the law of the land, their rights, and their duties. Above all, we keep it personal: honest feedback, steady mentorship, and accountability from your first class to your final selection.
