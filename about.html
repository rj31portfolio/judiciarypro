About JudiciaryPRO

JudiciaryPRO is the next chapter of a journey that began in 2013 when Sparsh Jain founded Chinar Law Institute in Gurugram and led it until the end of 2023. Over a decade, that classroom-first ethos, clarity in concepts, discipline in practice, and personal mentorship produced 50+ selections and a community of serious learners.

We carry that legacy forward with a modern hybrid model (online + offline), daily doubt-clearing, mains answer writing, weekly tests, bilingual teaching (English/Hindi), library access, and a current-affairs routine.
